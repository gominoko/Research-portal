Sarrat National High School, located in Brgy. 4 San Francisco, Sarrat, Ilocos Norte, is recognized for its dedication to providing quality education to learners. It holds the distinction of being the first public school to introduce the Special Program for Mathematics (SPM) in Ilocos Norte, a pioneering initiative conducted in collaboration with the Department of Science and Technology (DOST). Embracing "Excellence is Our Way of Life" as its tagline, Sarrat NHS prides itself on nurturing a culture of academic, personal, and overall excellence within the school community.

MISSION


SARRAT NHS dedicates itself to:


  • 1. Empower learners to be morally upright equipped with knowledge and skills ready to contribute and become agents of societal transformation to succeed in rapidly changing world.
  • 2. Use contextualized curriculum through integration of environmental awareness, research, robotics, entrepreneurship, and ICT innovations
  • 3. Bring out what's best in all employees to become highly effective teachers focused on improving learners' outcomes through their commitment to professional development, quality teaching. coaching, and mentoring with the collaborative support of highly competent administrative staff.
  • 4. Inspire a strong sense of commitment with partner institutions-LGUS, NGOs. PTA, alumni, and other stakeholders of the school,
  • 5. Support and sustain all programs and projects of the school especially Brigada Eskwela, Green School Project and Livelihood Program for Parents.
  • 6. Adhere all government rules and regulations and to practice fairness and transparency for customer service satisfaction.
  • 7. Above all, practice excellence as a way of life for the Glory of God.
